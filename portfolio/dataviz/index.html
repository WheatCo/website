Case Study: Data Visualization

When The Simple Dollar came to us, they were looking for a team of frontend engineers and designers to create an embeddable debt payoff calculator. We've now built them almost a dozen calculators of all sorts, including federal tax rates, state tax rates, auto loans, and a couple other types of interactive graphics.

In addition to design and development, we implemented the financial algorithms and researched statistics in-house.

We always turn to Wheat Co when we need excellent interactive graphics — they produce content quickly, and at reasonable rates. – The Simple Dollar

We used the Mithril library to lay out elements and live-update calculations from user input. We regularly use frontend DOM-diffing libraries like Mithril or React for projects like this. The charts were done in D3, with custom styling. We also used our own in-house layout library, Flexblocks.

To this day, we continue to provide additional interactives and support to The Simple Dollar.
